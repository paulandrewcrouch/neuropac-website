What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They help websites function, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

How we use cookies

NEUROPAC uses cookies for essential site functionality and analytics. We do not use cookies to collect personal information or for advertising purposes.
